⚜️ Game of Thrones ⚜️

A Crusader Kings III Mod

Crusader Kings 3 - A Game of Thrones is a total conversion mod set in the world of George R. R. Martin’s A Song of Ice and Fire fantasy saga, where lords great and small vie for control over the lands of Ice and Fire. The mod takes its name from the first book in the series, as well as the 'game' of plots and politics the ambitious and hopeful are said to be playing in their struggles for supremacy.
