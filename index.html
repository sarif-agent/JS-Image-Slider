A Crusader Kings III Mod

Crusader Kings 3 - A Game of Thrones is a total conversion mod set in the world of George R. R. Martin’s A Song of Ice and Fire fantasy saga, where lords great and small vie for control over the lands of Ice and Fire. The mod takes its name from the first book in the series, as well as the 'game' of plots and politics the ambitious and hopeful are said to be playing in their struggles for supremacy.

Mega Wars: Experience immersive, realm-wide wars like never before. The Mega Wars system expands opportunities for lesser vassals to take what is rightfully theirs. Players whose lieges go to war can take sides or remain neutral. Rebels who fail in their aspirations can face punishments ranging from losing their titles or watching their dynasty vanish before their very eyes. Chaos is a ladder, its rungs wrought by the complicated system of alliances and sympathies to lords high and low. ...
